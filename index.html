The Health Checklist

This is a company having the objective of simplifying the way patients and doctors keep their medical records and focusing on easy access to the records when needed.

About Us

Post the Covid Pandemic there are fears that one may contract the infection by touching surfaces such as prescriptions and reports, so we aim to digitalize this process by the use of technology to store all medical records on a cloud which can be easily retrieved via an app.
The Hon. Prime Minister in his address to the nation on 15th August 2020 also spoke about the need to digitalize medical records which will help in lessening the burden on the healthcare system. Taking into consideration the above factors, we provide a platform for both doctors and patients to store medical records and access them easily saving time and money.
